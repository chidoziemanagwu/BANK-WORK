Checking that works for you

Make banking easier with checking account options that help simplify everyday purchases, streamline bill paying and keep your funds secure.

Your banking needs are unique. How you use your checking account should be too. At Cathay Bank, our range of checking accounts give you the flexibility to bank the way you want, and offer the convenience and security to make purchases, pay bills and transfer funds with confidence.

Simplifying the way you save

Our savings accounts provide the convenience, ease and flexibility to help you manage your savings on your terms.

Whether you’re saving for a child’s college fund, a vacation or your next big purchase, Cathay Bank offers a variety of savings accounts designed to fit your lifestyle and financial goals. We offer competitive rates and added benefits to help you get more out of your savings account, along with free mobile banking to ensure your savings are always within reach.

Flexible and convenient lending

From large purchases like a home to unexpected expenses, our Personal Lending solutions can help you access the funds you need to achieve your personal and financial goals.

Cathay Bank offers flexible lending options. Whether you want to purchase a home or apply for a credit card, our products can fit your needs and goals. Our Personal Lending solutions feature competitive rates and flexible repayment terms, plus an easy application process that gives you quick access to your funds upon approval.

Access your accounts anywhere, anytime

Securely manage your accounts at home, at work and on the go.

Cathay Bank’s digital banking services make accessing and managing your accounts from your computer or mobile device easy and convenient. Whether you need to check your balance, pay bills, or transfer money, our digital banking services help you track and manage your finances everywhere you’re connected.
